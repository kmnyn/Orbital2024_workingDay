About MonoJar

Gen Z young adults these days are overwhelmed by stress. Highly competitive job market, increasing social expectations, and a rapidly changing professional landscape all contribute to various sources of stress. The rising incidence of mental health issues among young adults worldwide has become a pressing societal problem.

In light of this, we want to raise awareness about their complex emotions and provide an outlet for them to relieve their stress.

That's why we have MonoJar! It is an emotion management application that takes the form of an imaginative jar, allowing users to store and manage their complex emotions.

---- MonoJar by Li Mengyan & Wang Jia
