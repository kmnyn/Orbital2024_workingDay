User Guide

Create Jar & Jar Library allow you to note down your happy and sad moments in words, so that you can refer back to them in the future! In the Jar Library, you can filter and delete your jars. If your jar content is very long, a 'Show More' button will appear to help you manage overflowing of content.

Time Capsule & Capusle Library allow you to write a time capsule letter to yourself. E-letters will be delivered to you in-app on the scheduled datetime so that you can observe your own growth across time! Upcoming Capsule also shows the datetime of upcoming capsules to remind you.

Mood Tracker allows you to put down your daily mood in the calendar. A line chart that analyses your weekly and monthly mood trend will be provided to you! This allows you to understand your change in mood better.

For personalisation purpose, you can change your Jar Design in Settings. We have plenty of designs for you to choose from!
